Step 1: Open Netlify Drop

Click the button above to open Netlify Drop in a new tab.

Step 2: Find Your dist Folder

The dist folder is located in your project directory:

/tmp/cc-agent/60342656/project/dist

If you can't access this path directly, ask your assistant to help you locate or download the dist folder.

Step 3: Drag and Drop

Drag the entire dist folder onto the Netlify Drop page.

Important: Drag the folder itself, not the files inside it!

Step 4: Get Your URL

After uploading, Netlify will give you a URL like:

https://random-name-123456.netlify.app

Your privacy policy will be at:

https://random-name-123456.netlify.app/privacy

Step 5: Use in Google Play Store

Copy the privacy policy URL and paste it into your Google Play Console when submitting your app!

Alternative: Use Netlify CLI (if logged in)

If you're already logged into Netlify CLI, you can deploy with:

npx netlify-cli deploy --prod --dir=dist
