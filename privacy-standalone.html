Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Welcome to WriteWave AI Studio ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our AI copywriting application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely)
  • Content Input: Text, prompts, and information you provide to generate AI copy
  • User Preferences: Your settings, brand voice preferences, and template favorites
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we never store full payment details)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, and generation frequency
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Analytics: Content performance metrics and usage statistics to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI copywriting services
  • Generate personalized content based on your inputs
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Enterprise-grade PostgreSQL database with encryption
  • Row Level Security (RLS): Each user can only access their own data
  • Encrypted Connections: All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • OpenAI: Your prompts are sent to OpenAI's API to generate content (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • Stripe: For secure payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correct: Update or correct your information
  • Export: Download your generated content
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails (service emails still apply)

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Generated Content: Stored in your history unless manually deleted
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance

7. Children's Privacy

WriteWave AI Studio is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. By using WriteWave AI Studio, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States.

9. AI Content and Ownership

  • Your Content: You retain all rights to content you input into our service
  • Generated Content: AI-generated content is provided to you for your use
  • Training Data: We do not use your content to train AI models

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and app functionality
  • Analytics: Basic usage analytics to improve our service
  • No Advertising: We do not use cookies for advertising purposes
